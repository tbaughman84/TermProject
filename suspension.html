Shelby GT350 and GT350R Suspension

The Shelby GT350 and GT350R come equipped with electronic Magneride struts. The all-new integrated driver control system allows selection of five different modes that change ABS, stability control, traction control, steering effort, throttle mapping, MagneRide tuning and exhaust settings, depending on driver preference, to achieve maximum performance. The GT350 and 350R brakes are two-piece cross-drilled iron discs mounted to aluminum hats. At the front are 394-millimeter rotors clamped by Brembo six-piston fixed calipers with integrated caliper bridges, while 380-millimeter rotors at the rear use four-piston calipers.
