Shelby GT350 and 350R

The 2015-2020 Ford Shelby GT350 and GT350R comes equipped with a 526hp 5.2L V8 engine mated to a Tremec 3160 6-speed transmission. It comes in a variety of colors from white to the famous Ford Performance Blue. You can also choose to add painted racing stripes down the center line of the car. The GT350R comes with a carbon fiber wheel package, rear seat delete and a/c delete so its ready for track days. You can add those options back for an added fee. Below is a list of your color options.

  • Twister Orange Tri-Coat
  • Grabber Lime
  • Rapid Red Metallic
  • Ford Performance Blue
  • Velocity Blue
